# Privacy Policy for SelfHelp

**Last updated: January 2025**

## Overview

SelfHelp is an anxiety relief app that provides breathing and grounding exercises. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to be transparent about our practices.

## Data Collection

**SelfHelp does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically, the app:
- Does not require an account or login
- Does not collect personal information
- Does not track your location
- Does not access your contacts, photos, or files
- Does not use analytics or tracking services
- Does not contain advertisements
- Does not share any data with third parties
- Works completely offline

## Permissions

SelfHelp does not require any special permissions to function. The app operates entirely on your device without internet access.

## Third-Party Services

SelfHelp does not integrate with any third-party services that could collect your data.

## Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, SelfHelp is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the new policy on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

---

*This app is designed to help you find calm, not to collect your data.*
